Chicken Tenders Recipe

Try the Chicken Tenders Recipe now.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast (cut into strips)
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional for spice)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (panko or regular)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup buttermilk (or regular milk)
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Chicken:

  • Cut the chicken breasts into strips. If they’re thick, you can slice them into thinner pieces.

2. Set Up Breading Stations:

  • In one shallow bowl, mix together the flour, paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using).
  • In a second bowl, beat the eggs and add the buttermilk.
  • In a third bowl, mix breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese.

3. Bread the Chicken:

  • Dip each chicken strip into the flour mixture, then into the egg mixture, and finally coat it well with the breadcrumb mixture. Press lightly to ensure it sticks.

4. Fry the Chicken:

  • Heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil in a deep skillet or pan over medium-high heat.
  • Fry the chicken tenders in batches until golden brown and crispy (about 3-4 minutes per side).
  • Once cooked, drain on paper towels

Shawarma Recipe

Try the Shawarma Recipe now.

Ingredients

For the Marinade:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 lbs chicken thighs (or chicken breast, if preferred)

For the Shawarma:

  • 4 pita bread or flatbreads
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Sliced tomatoes and cucumbers
  • Pickles (optional)

For the Garlic Sauce (Tahini Sauce):

  • 1/2 cup tahini
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and water to thin (as needed)

Instructions

1. Marinate the Chicken:

  • In a bowl, combine yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, cumin, paprika, turmeric, coriander, cinnamon, garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
  • Add the chicken to the marinade and coat it well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.

2. Cook the Chicken:

  • Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium heat.
  • Cook the chicken for about 6-7 minutes per side until fully cooked and browned on the outside (internal temperature should reach 165°F or 75°C).
  • Once cooked, let the chicken rest for a few minutes, then slice it into thin strips.

3. Assemble the Shawarma:

  • Warm the pita or flatbread in a pan or microwave.
  • Add a layer of chicken slices, followed by onion, parsley, tomatoes, cucumbers, and pickles (if desired).
  • Drizzle with garlic sauce or tahini sauce.
